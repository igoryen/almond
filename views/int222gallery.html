Mt. Akhun

This is the view off Mt. Akhun. Mt. Akhun is a popular destination for people on a weekend and for couples on their wedding day. An urban legend is that on a fine day you can see across the Black Sea all the way to Turkey!

Sochi Mountains

Sochi is located between the Black Sea and the mountain ridge called the Caucasus. This is a view from a valley called Beautiful (Krasnaya Polyana). It is known as the Switzerland of Russia. A favorite destination for tourists due to its breathtaking scenery.
